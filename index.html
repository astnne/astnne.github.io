How to make homemade cheese

  1. Heat up 2L of milk and stur constantly
  2. Before milk boils, reduce heat to medium
  3. Add 6Tbsp of white vinegar into heated milk,
    little at a time, continue to stir after each addition
  4. Milk should seperate into curds and whey
  5. Remove from heat, let mixture sit for 15 minutes
    for seperation process to complete
  6. Seperate curds from whey and excess liquid using a colander
  7. Let curds sit for 1 hour
  8. Complete
